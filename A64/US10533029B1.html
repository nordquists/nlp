Abstract

Platinum(II) complexes with various selenones (L) having the general formula PtL2(NH3)2 +2 X−2 are disclosed. The platinum(II) complexes inhibit growth of cancer cells in vitro and are useful for treatment of proliferative disorders such as cancers and/or tumors.
