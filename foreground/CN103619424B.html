Abstract

The object of this invention is to provide table tennis rubber, the direction of ball can be controlled with it and ball can be made more easily to rotate, and the speed of ball can be increased with it.The present invention is table tennis rubber, and it comprises spongy and the laminated and sheet rubber sticked on spongy, wherein sheet rubber main body treat with the spongy surface adhered on form multiple projection.The feature of described table tennis rubber is that the thickness of described sheet rubber main body on laminated direction is 0.60mm or less.According to this table tennis rubber, the minimize energy losses of ball can be made, make can gather way and verticity, also can improve ball control performance.

Technical field
The present invention relates to soldier's pang the ball rubber used adhering to ping-pong bat main body.
Background technology
Table tennis rubber generally comprise spongy and with the spongy sheet rubber adhered to.The batting performance of table tennis rubber and ping-pong bat is closely related.
Particularly, table tennis rubber needs to improve speed ability (batting to return the performance of ball faster), verticity (performance that sportsman easily makes ball rotate) and control performance (sportsman is exactly with the performance of anticipated orientation batting).
Therefore, in order to improve these performances, proposed the multiple rubber with different component and surface profile.As one of this rubber, be called that the rubber of reverse sponge rubber (reversedspongerubber) is known.
The sheet rubber that reverse sponge rubber comprises spongy and is laminated on spongy.Sheet rubber has and is treating and multiple projections of being formed on the spongy surface adhered to herein.Reverse sponge rubber is furnished with flat hitting area to expand the contact area with ball.Which increase verticity.Meanwhile, reduce by projection being shifted onto the compression stiffness making whole rubber in spongy.This also extends with the time of contact of ball to increase control performance.
On the other hand, in reverse sponge rubber, be pulled in spongy in square stance projection.This is intended to reduce elastic force, causes the energy loss of ball.The energy loss of ball undesirably reduces the speed of ball.
Therefore, the table tennis rubber by regulating the character of the rubber being used as material to propose the speed ability of control performance and verticity and the improvement with increase.Such as, the table tennis rubber of the verticity that chemicals by giving from excellent elastic force to fusion in sheet rubber or mixing adhesion material improve is proposed routinely.
In addition, the table tennis rubber of the speed ability of the convex shape improvement had by modifying sheet rubber is proposed routinely.

As the result of further investigation, the present inventor find thickness X be 0.60mm or more I reduce the energy loss of ball at square stance.
