Abstract

The invention relates to an extensible table tennis bat. In order to effectively expand the function of a table tennis bat and achieve the purposes that the table tennis bat can also serve as a simple badminton bat, an original table tennis bat handle is specially designed to be a handle capable of being extended and shortened, and the handle can be extended and shortened according to actual needs. The extensible table tennis bat is specially suitable for being used by children at different ages, and the problem that a child toy is single in structural function is solved.
