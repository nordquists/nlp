Abstract

The invention discloses a palm-type ping-pong bat and relates to ping-pong bats.The palm-type ping-pong bat comprises a bottom board and rubber covers, and is characterized in that a kidney-shaped or elliptical finger hole is formed in one end of a long shaft of the elliptical bottom board, a handle is arranged at the thickened edge of the outer end of the finger hole, the bottom board and the handle are integrated, and the rubber covers are arranged on two sides of the bottom board.The palm-type ping-pong bat is skillful in structure, stable to hold, flexible to use, simple, practical, capable of reducing exercise-induced injury maximally and conforming to a new trend of current ping-pong ball technological development and easy to learn, thereby being a preferred bat for national ping-pong ball learning and having great popularization value.
