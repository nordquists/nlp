Abstract

The invention discloses a novel table tennis ball tube. The novel table tennis ball tube comprises an upper tube body and a lower tube body, wherein a groove and an inner thread are formed in the upper tube body; an outer thread is formed in the lower tube body; the inner thread and the outer thread are matched with each other to connect the upper tube body and the lower tube body into a whole body; a handle is arranged on a table tennis bat; the groove matches with the handle. According to the novel table tennis ball tube, the table tennis ball tube and the table tennis bats are connected into a whole body, and a plurality of table tennis balls can be put into the table tennis ball tube in the table tennis playing process, not only saves space, but also is convenient to carry.
