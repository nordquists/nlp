Abstract

The present invention provides a kind of racket plank preparation technology, comprises the following steps：Former paulownia timber is taken, drying and shaping is carried out under the first preset temperature, drying original plank is obtained；The drying original plank is processed into template；The template and fiber are bonded, adhesive plate is obtained；The adhesive plate is subjected to drying and shaping under the second preset temperature, racket plank is produced.By the present invention in that with after former paulownia timber drying and shaping, being bonded, being carried out again after drying and shaping with fiber, racket plank is obtained, finished product racket intensity height, good toughness is obtained, anti-fight effect is good, it is to avoid existing racket plank final finished difference of hardness, the defect of anti-strike ability difference.
