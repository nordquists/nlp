Abstract

The present invention provides a kind of countable table tennis bat, described table tennis bat is built-in with gravity sensor, and in ball pommel provided with a small display, so when table tennis touches racket face, inductor will show a numeral by display screen, to reach the purpose of counting.

Description

Countable table tennis bat
Technical field
The present invention relates to a kind of racket, specifically a kind of countable table tennis bat.
Background technology
All like playing table tennis in usually study now with many pupils, due to begining a theatrical performance to be typically all with racket to weigh ball in the hand during exercise, because racket can not be counted, in order to reach it is certain weigh ball quantity in the hand, when every time practising, be all oneself to count, it is very inconvenient.
The content of the invention
It is an object of the invention to provide a kind of countable table tennis bat, table tennis bat is built-in with counter, and us can be helped to measure quantity of playing ball.
The present invention includes table tennis bat, and the technical scheme used is：The clappers of described table tennis bat are built-in with gravity sensor, can contact racket number of times to count by table tennis, and the small display on racket pommel is provided with one, can display counting.
The beneficial effects of the present invention are：Because table tennis bat is built-in with gravity sensor, and in ball pommel provided with a small display, so when table tennis touches racket face, inductor will show a numeral by display screen, to reach the purpose of counting.
