Abstract

The invention discloses a kind of Multifunctional table tennis bat, the product mainly includes：Racket, dismantled and assembled long handle, dismantled and assembled short handle, the screw that is connected, sealing ring.The present invention realizes the dual-purpose function of a bat by dismantled and assembled long handle or short handle.Wherein, short handle is directed at threaded locating hole, and tightens the screw that is connected, cover sealing ring, be achieved that the function of pen-hold grip；Similarly, long handle is directed at threaded locating hole, and tightens the screw that is connected, be achieved that the function of shake-hands grip.In a manner of simply and easily dismounting, the difference in functionality of same racket is realized, on the premise of the batting degree of accuracy is not influenceed, the utilization rate to material is substantially increased, while having saved the cost of enterprises and individuals, also strengthens the environmental consciousness of people.
