Abstract

The present invention provides a kind of plastic ping-pong rackets of removable hand perspiration, including clappers, handle, activated carbon adsorption component, fan component and end cap；Activated carbon adsorption component is arranged in cylinder shape groove, including shell and guide cylinder；Fan component includes fan, rotating bar, support plate and motor；End cap is connected by the rear end of bolt and handle.Compared with prior art, the beneficial effects of the present invention are：Overall structure is novel, it is not only good to the adsorption effect of hand perspiration, and activated carbon adsorption component, the fan component in handle all can be taken off being replaced and being cleaned, ensureing the effect of racket removal hand perspiration simultaneously, extend the service life of racket, whole production cost and difficulty of processing are low, have a vast market foreground.
