Abstract

The invention provides a table tennis bat, which is provided with an upper rubber surface, a lower rubber surface and a bottom plate, wherein the bottom plate is provided with a handle, an upper plate, a lower plate and a middle area, and is characterized in that: the middle area is arranged between the upper plate and the lower plate, and the handle is provided with a vent groove. The invention can stand the racket on the table surface, can effectively increase the contact area between the bottom plate and the air after the bottom plate is wetted, realizes the most efficient self-drying process and avoids the risk of damaging the bottom plate due to the fact that the bottom plate is in a wetted state for a long time.

Description

Table tennis bat
Technical Field
The invention relates to the field of sports equipment, in particular to a table tennis bat.
Background
The table tennis bat is a bat for hitting balls in table tennis, and consists of rubber and a bottom plate, after the bottom plate is wetted by water, the table tennis bat cannot achieve the purpose of effective self-drying, and can only be transversely placed, and cannot effectively stand on a table top.
