Abstract

The invention discloses a kind of bat of table tennis, including face, handle, it is characterized in that, in the bottom of handle, one duck eye is set, and installation can be closed the cover plate at duck eye hole, the mesh bag of the fixed dress table tennis in duck eye bottom outside duck eye, and mesh bag opening is equipped with bungee.Beneficial effect is: due to being equipped with duck eye, cover plate, mesh bag in table tennis bat handle bottom, table tennis can be put into mesh bag when not in use, bungee can prevent table tennis from dropping out, table tennis can be taken out from mesh bag when playing ball, mesh bag is filled in the duck eye of handle bottom, and hole is closed with cover plate, and it is simple and quick, it is very convenient to carry storage.
