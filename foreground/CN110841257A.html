Abstract

The invention provides a table tennis training bat and a table tennis rapid-forming training device, which comprise a training bat, a training ball, a rebound board matched and connected with the training ball and a support piece for fixing the training ball, wherein the table tennis training bat comprises a handle and a transverse board connected with the handle, the transverse board is a triangular board, one end of the triangular board is connected with an arc buckling and killing board, the outer end of the arc buckling and killing board is connected with an arc twisting and pulling board, and a crescent board with a hollow middle part, a flat upper side and a bent side is formed. The trainee strikes the ball on the vertical plate, rebounds back, continues to strike, and lets the ball fall on the transverse plate, the arc-shaped buckling plate or the arc-shaped twisting plate of the racket, and can complete the training of rubbing, splitting, cutting, chopping, pulling, twisting and the like without picking up the ball and a table, so that the trainee can rapidly master the skill and skill of the table tennis, improve the technical level of the table tennis as soon as possible, and meet the training requirement of the table tennis.

Technical Field
The invention relates to a table tennis training bat and a table tennis rapid training device, and belongs to the technical field of table tennis training equipment.
Background
At present, the product of self-training table tennis mainly has table tennis service robot or carries out the equipment trained with the help of current table tennis table, table tennis net, table tennis bat, but current training equipment all exists:
1) the device occupies a large training space, has a complex structure and high cost, and has the problem of large investment on ordinary families, units and schools; 2) the technical action is single, the key and skill for hitting the table tennis ball are difficult to master for the younger children, and the optimal golden training period of the children is influenced; 3) the auxiliary functions of ball-serving training and various ball-serving skills are lacked; 4) more training time is wasted to collect and pick up the hit table tennis. 5) Because the existing table tennis bat and table tennis are standard parts, for beginners with weak bases, when the beginners are buckled and play the table tennis, the played table tennis can be powerless or suddenly rises out of the bound when the middle part of the table tennis bat is used for playing the table tennis, and ideal buckling strength is difficult to obtain, so that trainees are difficult to master the table tennis playing skill, the technical level is delayed, the speed is improved, and the training quality is finally influenced.
The table tennis is a good aerobic exercise, is suitable for healthy exercises of teenagers and children as well as middle-aged and old people, and is an optimal item for promoting eye exercises. At present, the decline of teenagers' physique is not only lack eye movement, still lacks cervical vertebra motion, whole body movement, consequently, does not have the exercise place, does not have under the condition of practicing the table, how to let this motion of table tennis develop widely, must research and develop a table tennis training articles for use that the function is complete.

Detailed Description
The invention is further described below with reference to the accompanying drawings.
The invention provides a table tennis training racket, which comprises a handle 1 and a transverse plate 2 connected with the handle 1, wherein the transverse plate 2 is a triangular plate, the handle 1 is fixed at the corner point of the triangular plate, the right end of the transverse plate 2 is connected with an arc buckling plate 3, the lower end of the arc buckling plate 3 is connected with an arc twisting plate 4, the horizontal end 5 of the triangle, the arc buckling plate 3 and the arc twisting plate 4 form a crescent plate with a hollow middle part, a flat upper side and a bent side, so that a batting or batting point falls on the transverse plate 2 or the arc buckling plate 3 or the arc twisting plate 4 corresponding to the training racket, and the training of batting, splitting, chipping, chopping, batting, smashing, pulling, table tennis and the like is realized, the skill and the level of a trainee are rapidly improved, and the table tennis training requirement is met.
The invention provides a table tennis rapid-forming training device, which comprises a training racket, a training ball 6, a rebound board matched with the training ball 6 and a support piece for fixing the training ball 6, wherein:
the training racket comprises a handle 1 and a transverse plate 2 connected with the handle 1, wherein the transverse plate 2 is a triangular plate, the handle 1 is fixed at the corner point of the triangular plate, the right end of the transverse plate 2 is connected with an arc buckling plate 3, the lower end of the arc buckling plate 3 is connected with an arc twisting plate 4, and a horizontal end 5 of the triangle, the arc buckling plate 3 and the arc twisting plate 4 form a crescent plate with a hollow middle part, a flat upper side and a bent side;
the training ball 6 is a microsphere with the diameter of 0.8-2 cm;
the rebound plate comprises a base 14 and a vertical plate 13 arranged on the base 14, and a movable wheel can be arranged below the base 14 to facilitate movement;
the support piece comprises a hollow horizontal rod 9 fixed at the top of the vertical plate 13 and a traction rope 7 arranged in the hollow of the horizontal rod 9, the outer end of the traction rope 7 droops through a first pulley 8 and then is connected with the training ball 6, and the inner end of the traction rope is fixed on the winding mechanism through a second pulley 10;
the winding mechanism comprises a reel 11, two ends of a wheel shaft of the reel 11 are respectively fixed on a bearing with a seat 15, the wheel shaft at the right end penetrates through the bearing with the seat 15 to extend outwards and then is connected with a handle 12, the bearing with the seat 15 is fixed on the back of a vertical plate 12 so as to operate the handle 12, drive the reel 11 to rotate to take in and release a traction rope 7, adjust the height of a training ball 6 and meet the training requirements of different trainees; the training ball 6 is a plastic hollow ball and is used for training various ball playing actions and enabling each ball to fall on the training racket.
During training, a trainee holds the training racket handle 1 to beat or kill the training ball 6 to the vertical plate 13, rebounds, and then beats or kills, so that training is carried out, the table tennis skill and level of the trainee are greatly improved, and the table tennis training requirement is met.
