Abstract

The invention provides a table tennis bat with a lamp, wherein a small LED lamp is arranged at the tail end of a handle of the table tennis bat, and when a user goes home after practicing playing a table tennis at night, the LED lamp on the handle can be turned on when the user walks on the road, so that the table tennis bat can be used for illumination, and children are prevented from walking and being afraid of black.

Description

Table tennis bat with lamp
Technical Field
The invention relates to a table tennis bat, in particular to a table tennis bat with a lamp.
Background
Some families can let children learn to play table tennis, and sometimes send the table tennis at night. When the student goes home after learning at night, the light on the road is dark, and people can fear when walking alone, and the student can carry on without an illuminating lamp.
Disclosure of Invention
The invention aims to provide a table tennis bat with a lamp, wherein the table tennis bat is provided with a small lamp and can be conveniently used for illumination.
The invention comprises a table tennis bat, and adopts the technical scheme that: the table tennis bat is provided with a small LED lamp at the tail end of a handle of the bat, is powered by a built-in battery and is provided with a control switch.
The invention has the beneficial effects that: because the tail end of the handle of the table tennis bat is provided with the LED small lamp, when people go home after practicing playing table tennis at night, the LED lamp on the handle can be turned on when people go on the road, so that the table tennis bat can be used for illumination, and children are prevented from walking dark.
