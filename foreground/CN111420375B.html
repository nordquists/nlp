Abstract

The invention discloses an ultralight amorphous reinforced carbon fiber table tennis bat, which comprises a core plate, a carbon fiber layer and an amorphous alloy layer; the core board is mainly made of foaming material; the carbon fiber layer is coated outside the core plate; the amorphous alloy layer is coated outside the carbon fiber layer. The surface of the table tennis bat of the product is provided with the multilayer amorphous alloy thin strip, and the table tennis bat has the characteristics of high elastic energy storage and moderate modulus, so that the bat bottom plate can rapidly generate corresponding deformation in low impact, the ball control is facilitated, and the defects of poor ball control performance and poor hand feeling caused by overlarge rigidity of the carbon fiber table tennis bat are thoroughly solved; when the ball is impacted greatly, the amorphous alloy has high elasticity and energy storage, so that the force can be more effectively transmitted to the table tennis through the rubber, the hitting efficiency is improved by more than 30%, and the ball is particularly suitable for attack methods such as a loop ball and the like.

Ultra-light amorphous reinforced carbon fiber table tennis bat and preparation method thereof
Technical Field
The invention relates to the technical field of sports equipment, in particular to an ultralight amorphous reinforced carbon fiber table tennis bat and a preparation method thereof.
Background
The base plate of the table tennis bat on the market at present still mainly takes light wood, has moderate performances such as density, elasticity and the like, gives consideration to comprehensive performances such as flexibility, ball control, speed and the like, and also accords with the consumption level of the public, so the base plate of the table tennis bat is the first choice for many years. Generally, the main wood of table tennis bat includes eucalyptus, forest wood, tung wood, spruce, basswood, juniper wood, walnut wood, etc., and its properties are all distinctive. However, the properties of various kinds of wood are greatly different, and natural factors such as growing regions, climatic conditions, whether rainwater is sufficient (water content) and the like have great influence on the characteristics of the wood; in addition, the cutting mode, the grain direction, the color difference, the knots, the mineral lines and the like of the wood in the processing process can greatly influence the performance of the racket, and the consistency of the performance of the wooden racket is seriously influenced.
With the continuous development of the table tennis level, the limitation that the elasticity and the strength of wood are insufficient is gradually highlighted, and the new requirements of higher hitting speed and stronger rotation cannot be met. Therefore, the multilayer laminated wood base plate and the interlayer made of new materials such as carbon fiber, aryl fiber and glass fiber with higher elastic modulus are added on the basis of the multilayer laminated wood base plate, and the development trend of the table tennis bat is formed. However, the new process is basically only modified on the basis of the original wood, only certain properties are improved to a certain extent, fundamental innovation is not provided, the weight is limited by the larger density of the wood material, the weight cannot be further reduced, and the transverse bat and the straight bat of the table tennis bat with the wood substrate are basically concentrated on 90-95 g and 85-90 g.
Currently, in order to further reduce the weight of the table tennis bat and improve the control flexibility and the hitting speed of the table tennis bat, manufacturers try to combine a foam core material with a carbon fiber composite structure. Among them, CN107415281A describes a method for preparing a carbon fiber table tennis bat by self-expansion molding, which uses an ultra-low density polymer with self-thermal expansion as an inner core material, provides the functions of shaping and supporting, and obtains a table tennis bat product with very light weight. CN201710788184.6 also mentions that a table tennis bat is integrally formed by adopting foaming filler and carbon fiber, and the table tennis bat with good elasticity and light weight is obtained. However, the materials and the process have a very big defect, when the impact is small, the carbon fiber modulus on the surface of the racket exceeds 250GPa, so that the rigidity is too high, the ball holding time is short, the vibration attenuation time is long, the vibration feedback time is long and scattered, and the ball control and the hand feeling of the technical means such as ball twisting and pendulum are very poor; and when the impact is great, the strength of the low-density core material is low, and effective support cannot be provided for the carbon fiber layer on the surface, so that the efficiency of attack modes such as killing, large-force toroidal balls and the like is very low.
The amorphous alloy is a new material with a unique atomic arrangement structure with long-range disorder and short-range order, the tensile strength of the amorphous alloy can reach 1800 plus 4000MPa or even higher, and the elastic modulus of the amorphous alloy is only about 70-80 GPa; more importantly, the elastic energy storage (elastic specific work) thereof exceeds 2.3 multiplied by 107J/m3And the elastic limit can reach 2%. In addition, the unique atomic structure of amorphous alloys also makes them insensitive to stress-notches and very fatigue resistant. Therefore, the modification of carbon fiber composite materials, cement-based composite materials and the like by utilizing the unique amorphous mechanical properties is a research hotspot in the field of the current composite materials.
Disclosure of Invention
In view of the above, the present invention provides an ultra-light amorphous reinforced carbon fiber table tennis bat and a preparation method thereof, which can effectively solve the disadvantages of poor ball control performance and poor hand feeling of the existing carbon fiber table tennis bat due to too high rigidity.
In order to achieve the purpose, the invention adopts the following technical scheme:
an ultralight amorphous reinforced carbon fiber table tennis bat comprises a core plate, a carbon fiber layer and an amorphous alloy layer; the core board is mainly made of foaming material; the carbon fiber layer is coated outside the core plate; the preparation method of the ultralight amorphous reinforced carbon fiber table tennis bat comprises the following steps:
(1) preparing an amorphous alloy thin strip: preparing an amorphous alloy thin strip by adopting a strip throwing and rapid quenching method;
(2) preparing a core plate: preparing a preformed core plate by mainly adopting a foaming material;
(3) pasting carbon fiber yarns: coating carbon fiber yarns containing 30-50% of epoxy resin by mass on the outer surface of the core plate according to an alternate 90-degree layering change mode, wherein the carbon fiber yarns are in even layers to form a preformed carbon fiber layer;
(4) attaching an amorphous alloy thin strip: coating epoxy resin on the amorphous strip thin strip, controlling the mass content of the epoxy resin to be 20-30%, firstly, sticking a layer of amorphous alloy thin strip on the two sides of the racket and the racket handle on the surface of the carbon fiber layer in the direction parallel to the racket handle; then the direction of 90 degrees is changed, namely the direction vertical to the racket handle, a layer of amorphous alloy thin strip is adhered on the two sides of the racket and the racket handle, the total number of layers of the amorphous alloy thin strip is even number, so as to form a preformed amorphous alloy layer;
(5) and (3) putting into a mould for curing and forming: putting the table tennis bat preform with the preformed amorphous alloy layer, carbon fiber layer and core plate composite structure into a mold, closing the mold, heating to 130-150 ℃, preserving the heat for 20-30 minutes, cooling and taking out to obtain a table tennis bat rough blank, and removing burrs, polishing and spraying paint to obtain the ultralight amorphous reinforced carbon fiber table tennis bat.
Preferably, the core plate comprises a main core body and at least one sub-core material, wherein mounting holes are formed in two side faces of the main core body in a penetrating mode, the sub-core material is embedded in the mounting holes, the sub-core material comprises a foam core body and a carbon fiber shell covering the foam core body, and two ends of the carbon fiber shell are integrally formed with the carbon fiber layer to form an I-shaped reinforcing beam.
Preferably, the foaming material is an ultra-low density foaming material or a high-energy glue type foaming material mixed with low-boiling-point alkane and having secondary foaming capability.
Preferably, the zirconium-based amorphous alloy with good elastic energy storage is selected for the amorphous alloy ribbon, and a transition metal element is added in a formula of the zirconium-based amorphous alloy, and the zirconium-based amorphous alloy comprises the following components in atomic percentage: 30-70% of Zr, 5-40% of Cu, 5-30% of Ni, 0-20% of Al and 0-10% of transition metal elements.
Preferably, the alloys prepared according to the formula ratio are placed in a vacuum furnace for repeated smelting for many times to obtain a master alloy with uniform components, then a copper roller is used as a mold to realize rapid strip throwing in a vacuum environment, an amorphous alloy thin strip with the thickness of 40-50 microns is obtained after rapid cooling, and then the cutting is carried out according to the actual process.
Preferably, in order to facilitate the subsequent pasting process, the amorphous ribbon thin strip with the width of 5-10 mm is cut for the racket handle area, the amorphous ribbon thin strip is cut for the racket face area, the amorphous ribbon thin strip is cut for the ball hitting face area to be more than 10 mm, or the whole racket face is cut out integrally.
Compared with the prior art, the invention has obvious advantages and beneficial effects, and specifically, the technical scheme includes that:
(1) the surface of the table tennis bat is provided with a plurality of layers of amorphous alloy thin strips, so that the table tennis bat has the characteristics of high elastic energy storage and moderate modulus, the bat bottom plate can rapidly generate corresponding deformation in low impact, the ball control is facilitated, and the defects of poor ball control performance and poor hand feeling caused by overlarge rigidity of the carbon fiber table tennis bat are thoroughly solved; when the ball is impacted greatly, the amorphous alloy has high elasticity and energy storage, so that the force can be more effectively transmitted to the table tennis through the rubber, the hitting efficiency is improved by more than 30%, and the ball is particularly suitable for attack methods such as a loop ball and the like.
(2) The I-shaped reinforcing beam is formed in the table tennis bat, so that the front surface and the back surface of the table tennis bat form an effective whole, the structural strength of the table tennis bat is greatly improved, and the fatal weakness that the back force is insufficient when the ball is vigorously hit due to the insufficient strength of the foaming core material of the current carbon fiber table tennis bat is avoided.
(3) Because the table tennis bat is internally made of the foaming material with very low density, the main framework is made of the carbon fiber composite material, and the thickness of the added amorphous ribbon thin strip is only 40-50 microns, on the premise of meeting the requirement of batting performance, the weight of the whole bat base plate can be reduced from 90-95 g of the traditional wood transverse bat, 85-90 g of the straight bat to 70-80g of the transverse bat and 65-75 g of the straight bat, so that the control flexibility of the bat is greatly improved.
(4) The amorphous alloy belt is added on the racket handle, and the characteristics of high elastic energy storage and moderate modulus of the amorphous alloy belt are utilized, so that when the racket is used for killing or pulling a loop ball and the like to hit a ball with a large force, the amorphous belt on the handle can generate resilience force due to rapid deformation after the handle is subjected to bending moment force, the hitting efficiency of the racket can be improved by more than 5%, and the amorphous alloy belt is different from the traditional wooden or carbon fiber racket handle.

Detailed Description
Referring to fig. 1 to 3, a specific structure of an ultralight amorphous reinforced carbon fiber table tennis bat according to a preferred embodiment of the present invention is shown, which includes a core 10, a carbon fiber layer 20, and an amorphous alloy layer 30.
The core board 10 is mainly made of foaming material; the carbon fiber layer 20 is coated outside the core plate 10; the amorphous alloy layer 30 is coated outside the carbon fiber layer 20. In this embodiment, the core 10 includes a main core 11 and at least one sub-core 12, the two side surfaces of the main core 11 are formed with mounting holes 111, the sub-core 12 is embedded in the mounting holes 111, the sub-core 12 includes a foam core 121 and a carbon fiber shell 122 covering the foam core 121, two ends of the carbon fiber shell 122 are integrally connected with the carbon fiber layer 20 to form an i-shaped reinforcing beam, so as to enhance the overall strength of the racket, and the number of the sub-cores 12 may be multiple, which is not limited; the foaming material is an ultra-low density foaming material (such as common foaming materials of PU, EVA and the like) or a high-energy glue type foaming material which is mixed with low-boiling-point alkane and has secondary foaming capability so as to keep the low weight of the racket.
The invention also discloses a preparation method of the ultralight amorphous reinforced carbon fiber table tennis bat, which comprises the following steps:
(1) preparing an amorphous alloy thin strip: preparing an amorphous alloy thin strip 31 by adopting a strip throwing and rapid quenching method; the amorphous alloy ribbon 31 is made of a zirconium-based amorphous alloy with good elastic energy storage, such as the disclosed zirconium-copper-nickel-aluminum system, and meanwhile, in order to further increase the supercooling degree of the amorphous alloy and optimize the amorphous forming capability thereof, a transition metal element is added in the formula, and the amorphous alloy ribbon comprises the following components in atomic percentage: 30-70% of Zr, 5-40% of Cu, 5-30% of Ni, 0-20% of Al and 0-10% of transition metal elements, such as: zr63.7Cu17.15Ni9.8Al7.35Er2(ii) a Repeatedly melting the alloys prepared according to the formula proportion in a vacuum furnace for many times to obtain a master alloy with uniform components, then rapidly spinning by taking a copper roller as a mold in a vacuum environment, rapidly cooling to obtain an amorphous alloy thin strip with the thickness of 40-50 microns, and then cutting according to an actual process; in order to facilitate the subsequent pasting process, the amorphous ribbon thin strip with the width of 5-10 mm is cut for the racket handle area, the amorphous ribbon thin strip with the width of 10 mm is cut for the racket surface area, or the whole racket surface is cut out.
(2) Preparing a core plate: the preformed core 10 is prepared mainly from a foam material.
(3) Pasting carbon fiber yarns: carbon fiber yarns containing 30-50% of epoxy resin in percentage by mass are coated on the outer surface of the core plate according to a layer change mode of alternating 90 degrees, the carbon fiber yarns are in even layers so as to ensure the symmetry of mechanical properties and form a preformed carbon fiber layer.
(4) Attaching an amorphous alloy thin strip: coating epoxy resin on the amorphous strip thin strip, controlling the mass content of the epoxy resin to be 20-30%, firstly, sticking a layer of amorphous alloy thin strip on the two sides of the racket and the racket handle on the surface of the carbon fiber layer in the direction parallel to the racket handle; then the direction of 90 degrees is changed, namely the direction vertical to the racket handle, a layer of amorphous alloy thin strip is adhered on the two surfaces of the racket and the racket handle, the total number of layers of the amorphous alloy thin strip is even number, so as to ensure the symmetry of the mechanical property, generally 4 or 6 layers can be formed, and a preformed amorphous alloy layer is formed.
(5) And (3) putting into a mould for curing and forming: putting the table tennis bat preform with the preformed amorphous alloy layer, carbon fiber layer and core plate composite structure into a mold, closing the mold, heating to 130-150 ℃, preserving the heat for 20-30 minutes, cooling and taking out to obtain a table tennis bat rough blank, and removing burrs, polishing and spraying paint to obtain the ultralight amorphous reinforced carbon fiber table tennis bat.
By adopting the structure and the preparation method, the structure has the following characteristics:
(1) the surface of the table tennis bat is provided with a plurality of layers of amorphous alloy thin strips, so that the table tennis bat has the characteristics of high elastic energy storage and moderate modulus, the bat bottom plate can rapidly generate corresponding deformation in low impact, the ball control is facilitated, and the defects of poor ball control performance and poor hand feeling caused by overlarge rigidity of the carbon fiber table tennis bat are thoroughly solved; when the ball is impacted greatly, the amorphous alloy has high elasticity and energy storage, so that the force can be more effectively transmitted to the table tennis through the rubber, the hitting efficiency is improved by more than 30%, and the ball is particularly suitable for attack methods such as a loop ball and the like.
(2) The I-shaped reinforcing beam is formed in the table tennis bat, so that the front surface and the back surface of the table tennis bat form an effective whole, the structural strength of the table tennis bat is greatly improved, and the fatal weakness that the back force is insufficient when the ball is vigorously hit due to the insufficient strength of the foaming core material of the current carbon fiber table tennis bat is avoided.
(3) Because the table tennis bat is internally made of the foaming material with very low density, the main framework is made of the carbon fiber composite material, and the thickness of the added amorphous ribbon thin strip is only 40-50 microns, on the premise of meeting the requirement of batting performance, the weight of the whole bat base plate can be reduced from 90-95 g of the traditional wood transverse bat, 85-90 g of the straight bat to 70-80g of the transverse bat and 65-75 g of the straight bat, so that the control flexibility of the bat is greatly improved.
(4) The amorphous alloy belt is added on the racket handle, and the characteristics of high elastic energy storage and moderate modulus of the amorphous alloy belt are utilized, so that when the racket is used for killing or pulling a loop ball and the like to hit a ball with a large force, the amorphous belt on the handle can generate resilience force due to rapid deformation after the handle is subjected to bending moment force, the hitting efficiency of the racket can be improved by more than 5%, and the amorphous alloy belt is different from the traditional wooden or carbon fiber racket handle.
