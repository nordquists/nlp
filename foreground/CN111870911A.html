Abstract

The invention discloses an outdoor ball and a racket. The ball comprises a ball body and a ball head which are fixedly connected, wherein the ball body sequentially comprises a spherical crown, a supporting rod and a base. The spherical crown is a horn-shaped integral curved surface formed by encircling a plurality of local curved surfaces, and each local curved surface is provided with a plurality of airflow holes; one end of each of the support rods is connected above the base, and the other end of each of the support rods is connected with the spherical crown; a cylindrical plug-in part is arranged below the base. The top surface plane of the ball head is downwards provided with a plug-in groove matched with the shape and the size of the plug-in part. The inserting part is inserted into the inserting groove and fixed to form an integral ball with a symmetrical central shaft. The racket comprises a racket frame, a handle and a racket board, wherein two end parts of the racket frame extend to the handle and are fixedly connected, the racket board is arranged in the racket frame, the racket board and the racket frame are externally coated with a surface skin layer, and a plurality of vent holes are formed in the penetrating surface skin layer and the racket board. The invention provides brand new outdoor sports equipment, which has certain badminton pleasure, small wind resistance and stable flight and is suitable for outdoor use.

Technical Field
The invention belongs to the technical field of sports equipment, in particular to an outdoor ball and a racket, and belongs to novel equipment used for outdoor sports.
Background
More and more people enjoy badminton, and the structure of the badminton determines that badminton is generally carried out indoors. One way, the participants in badminton are not willing to be professional athletes, much more for exercise and entertainment purposes. Because the indoor playground is limited and limited by the field, participants need to reserve the field in advance and can arrive through a section of road, and the participation enthusiasm is influenced.
With the development of cities, the number of urban greens is increased, such as community gardens, community parks, complex squares and the like. At present, most of the exercises suitable for outdoor use are walking, roller skating, square dance and the like. These sports are not suitable for all people, and there is a great gap in outdoor sports equipment.
The utility model with the patent number of 201120294210.8 discloses a windproof badminton, which is an outdoor sports equipment similar to badminton and comprises an elastic ball and feathers, and the elastic ball and the feathers are connected by a wire. Although the wind resistance of the badminton is small, the badminton is unstable in flight and not ideal in use feeling.

Detailed description of the preferred embodiments
The present invention will be described in further detail with reference to the accompanying drawings and specific embodiments.
An outdoor ball and a racket, the adopted ball is similar to a badminton, the adopted racket is similar to a beach racket, and the outdoor ball and the racket are a new outdoor sport.
Example 1.
As shown in fig. 1 and 2, an outdoor ball comprises a ball body 1 and a ball head 2.
The integrally formed sphere 1 sequentially comprises a spherical cap 11, a support rod 12 and a base 13. The spherical cap 11 is a trumpet-shaped integral curved surface formed by surrounding a plurality of local curved surfaces, and each local curved surface is provided with a plurality of airflow holes 111. The airflow holes 111 are in the shape of a strip, a circle, a rectangle or a triangle, and the airflow holes in this embodiment adopt a combination of a strip, a rectangle and a triangle. The curved surface shape of each partial curved surface, and the shape and layout of the airflow holes 111 are the same.
The six struts 12 are uniformly distributed along the central axis, one end of each strut 12 is converged above the base 13, and the other end of each strut 12 extends to the spherical crown 11 to form a supporting rib 112 of the spherical crown part. The supporting ribs evenly divide the horn-shaped overall curved surface into six local curved surfaces. Two adjacent local curved surfaces have different heights at two sides close to the common supporting rib 112. In this embodiment, the curved surface of the latter portion is higher than the curved surface of the former portion on both sides of the support rib in the clockwise direction. In order to reduce the weight of the ball, the cross-sectional shape of the support rod 12 may be V-shaped, Y-shaped, U-shaped, E-shaped, T-shaped or cross-shaped, and in this embodiment, T-shaped is adopted.
The bottom of the base 13 has a cylindrical plug portion 131 with a circular or regular polygonal cross-section. Correspondingly, the plane of the top surface of the ball head 2 is provided with a plug-in groove 21 which is matched with the plug-in part 131 in shape downwards, and the shape matching can be an annular groove which is matched with the plug-in part 131 in shape or a cylindrical groove of which the inner wall is matched with the outer wall of the plug-in part 131 in shape. In this embodiment, a circular groove matching the shape and size of the insertion portion 131 is used.
As shown in fig. 3, the ball head 2 is a segment body with segment height H larger than ball radius R, which increases the contact area between the ball and the bat and enlarges the hitting angle; the inserting groove 21 and the spherical segment body are the same central axis.
The inserting part 131 of the ball body is inserted into the inserting groove 21 of the ball head, the ball body 1 is fixedly connected with the ball head 2, and the whole ball body is symmetrical to the central axis.
Example 2.
As shown in fig. 4, the outdoor ball comprises a ball body 1 and a ball head 2. The ball 1 comprises a spherical cap 11, a strut 12 and a base 13.
Spherical crown 11 integrated into one piece, including one by a plurality of local curved surfaces enclose synthetic tubaeform whole curved surface 11 and six peg graft poles 113, peg graft pole 113 upwards extends, forms spherical crown curved surface brace rod, and the brace rod evenly divide into six local curved surfaces with tubaeform whole curved surface, and it has a plurality of airflow holes 111 to open on every local curved surface, and the airflow hole on every local curved surface adopts non-uniform distribution. The curved surface shape of each partial curved surface, and the shape and layout of the airflow holes 111 are the same. The heights of two adjacent local curved surfaces at two sides close to the shared supporting rib are different.
The heads of the six supporting rods 12 are provided with inserting pipes 121, and a connecting rib 123 is arranged between every two adjacent supporting rods 12. The connecting ribs 123 can prevent the support rod from shaking in flight, and the action of the connecting ribs is similar to that of a coil on a feather rod of a traditional badminton. A cylindrical plug-in part 131 is arranged below the base 13, and a plug-in groove 21 matched with the plug-in part 131 in shape and size is formed downwards on the plane of the top surface of the ball head 2. All the struts 12, the inserting pipes 121, the connecting ribs 123 and the base 13 are integrally formed to form a spherical crown frame.
The inserting rod 113 of the spherical cap is inserted into the inserting pipe 121 corresponding to the spherical cap frame, and is solidified by adding glue to form the sphere 1. The inserting part 131 of the ball body is inserted into the inserting groove 21 of the ball head, the ball body 1 is fixedly connected with the ball head 2, and the whole ball body is symmetrical to the central axis.
