Abstract

The invention provides a picket racket edge protection strip which comprises an edge strip body, wherein the edge strip body covers the end face of a racket, the edge strip body comprises side edges and flanges, the flanges are arranged on two sides of the side edges and are in a vertical structure with the side edges, the tail ends of the flanges are in an inclined plane structure, and the inner walls of the flanges are tightly adhered to the upper surface and the lower surface of the edge of the racket. The strake body adopts the plastics material to make through injection mold integration. The edge strip body is symmetrical from the upper part of the handle of the racket to the upper part of the other side of the handle of the racket by surrounding the racket. The outer surface of the joint of the side edge and the retaining edge is arc-shaped. The side edge and the flange of the edge strip are tightly attached to the racket. The integrally formed picket racket edge protection strip made of the plastic material through the injection mold is simple in structure, convenient to manufacture and low in cost, and the edge protection strip made of the plastic material is good in abrasive resistance and clamping degree, convenient to install by workers, convenient to replace and capable of reducing the defective rate.

Technical Field
The invention relates to the technical field of sports goods, in particular to a picket racket edge protection strip.
Background
Pickball is a sport that hits balls with a racket, which has emerged from the islands of the united states of seattle, a mixed sport that combines tennis, badminton, and table tennis. The existing rubber edge protecting strip for the pickles has the problems of complex structure and production operation and high defective rate, and can not meet the production requirement.

Detailed Description
The invention is further described below with reference to the accompanying drawings.
Example 1: the edge protecting strip for the picket rackets as shown in fig. 1 to 5 comprises an edge body 1, wherein the edge body 1 covers the end surface of the racket 2, the edge body 1 comprises a side edge 11 and a flange 12, the flange 12 is arranged on two sides of the side edge 11 and is of a vertical structure with the side edge 11, the tail end of the flange 12 is of an inclined plane structure, and the inner wall of the flange 12 is tightly adhered to the upper surface and the lower surface of the edge of the racket 2.
The utility model discloses a racket, including racket 2, side 11, flange 12, edge strip body 1, the handle top of racket 2 is wound around racket 2 to the opposite side top of racket 2 handle and is the symmetry, the outside surface of the junction of side 11 and flange 12 is circular-arcly, the side 11 and the flange 12 of edge strip all closely laminate with racket 2.
In the invention, the length of the side edges 11 at the two ends of the edge strip body 1 is less than that of the baffle 12.
The size of the edge strip body 1 is integrally formed according to various types, so that the attaching degree of the edge strip body 1 and the racket 2 is guaranteed, and great convenience is provided for subsequent replacement.
The edge strips are convenient to mount on the racket 2, and the integral structure is integrally manufactured, so that the integral replacement is convenient when the replacement is carried out.
The integrally formed picket racket 2 protective edge strip made of plastic through the injection mold is simple in structure, convenient to manufacture and low in cost, the edge strip made of the plastic is good in abrasive resistance and clamping degree, installation of workers is facilitated, replacement is convenient and rapid, and defective rate is reduced.
