The invention relates to an anti-extrusion light table tennis bat sleeve, which comprises: first bat cover, second bat cover, first bat cover with the opening of second bat cover passes through the zip fastener and connects, first bat cover with all be provided with the air chamber in the second bat cover. Technical Field
The invention belongs to the technical field of table tennis bat sleeves, and particularly relates to an anti-extrusion light table tennis bat sleeve.
Background
Table tennis is a popular ball sport in the world, and people usually use a bat sleeve to contain the table tennis bat in order to protect the table tennis bat. The table tennis bat cover used by people at present, or a simple cover body made of nylon fiber or leather, has no anti-extrusion device, or has a plastic plate on which the table tennis bat can not be fixed, and can not effectively prevent the table tennis bat from being extruded, damaged or even broken, thereby bringing inconvenience and trouble to users.

Detailed Description
The preferred embodiments of the present invention will be described in conjunction with the accompanying drawings, and it will be understood that they are described herein for the purpose of illustration and explanation and not limitation.
The invention provides an anti-extrusion light table tennis bat sleeve, as shown in figures 1-2, comprising: first bat cover 1, second bat cover 2, first bat cover 1 with the opening that the second clapped cover 2 passes through zip fastener 3 to be connected, first bat cover 1 with all be provided with air chamber 4 in the second bat cover 2.
The working principle and the beneficial technical effects of the technical scheme are as follows: put the table tennis bat between first bat cover 1 and second bat cover 2, draw zipper 3, when the lateral surface of first bat cover 1 and second bat cover 2 received the extrusion of external force, the effect of buffering external force can be played to the air that is full of in the air chamber 4 to play the protection table tennis bat, prevent that the bat extrusion from damaging, even the rupture.
In one embodiment, as shown in fig. 2, the inner side surfaces of the first racket case 1 and the second racket case 2 are provided with placing grooves 5, and the placing grooves 5 are similar to the shape of a racket, so that the racket can be placed conveniently.
In one implementation, the side of first bat cover 1 is provided with trachea 6, trachea 6 one end with air chamber 4 intercommunication, trachea 6 other end extends outside first bat cover 1 to with lid 7 threaded connection, open lid 7, can fill into the air through trachea 6 to air chamber 4, make bat cover have good buffering effect, play the effect of protection racket.
In one embodiment, the outer wall of the lid 7 is provided with anti-slip threads, which increase friction and prevent slipping when the lid is turned.
In one implementation, the first racket case 1 and the second racket case 2 are pear-shaped, so that edges and corners are reduced, and a user is prevented from being scratched.
In one embodiment, as shown in fig. 3, an anti-squeezing device is disposed on an outer wall of the first racket case 1, and the anti-squeezing device includes: a bottom plate 8, a pressure plate 9, a first spring 11, a second spring 14, a third spring 15,
a bottom plate 8 is arranged on the outer side surface of the first racket sleeve 1, a pressing plate 9 is arranged above the bottom plate 8, and two ends of the bottom plate 8 are connected with two ends of the pressing plate 9 through flexible cloth 10;
the bottom plate 8 with be connected through a plurality of first springs 11 between the clamp plate 9, bottom plate 8 with be provided with drum 12 between the clamp plate 9, drum 12 lower extreme with bottom plate 8 middle department is connected, be provided with cylinder 13 in the drum 12, cylinder 13 can be in reciprocating motion from top to bottom in the drum 12, second spring 14 one end with cylinder 13 upper end is connected, the second spring 14 other end with the clamp plate 9 is connected, be provided with third spring 15 in the second spring 14, third spring 15 one end with the clamp plate 9 is connected, the third spring 15 other end with cylinder 13 upper end is connected, second spring 14 is in compression state, third spring 15 is in tensile state.
The working principle and the beneficial technical effects of the technical scheme are as follows: when the racket sleeve is subjected to smaller impact force, the pressing plate 9 can be extruded by external force, and at the moment, the first spring 11 and the second spring 14 play a role in buffering, so that the bottom plate 8 and the first racket sleeve 1 are protected, and the flat racket is prevented from being extruded and damaged; when the bat cover receives great impact force, along with the continuous pushing down of clamp plate 9, third spring 15 can be changed into compression state by tensile state, leads to whole buffer structure's rigidity to increase by a wide margin to can deal with great impact force, prevent that the table tennis bat from damaging.
In one example, as shown in fig. 3, the pressure plate 9 is provided with a plurality of ventilation holes 16, which facilitate the air discharge between the bottom plate 8 and the pressure plate 9, and prevent the flexible cloth 10 from being broken due to the excessive impact of external force.
In one example, the air holes 16 are provided with a filter screen 17, and the filter screen 17 can filter air to prevent dust or foreign matters from entering into a space between the bottom plate 8 and the pressing plate 9.
In one example, as shown in fig. 4-5, a guiding hole 18 is formed in the outer wall of the cylinder 12 in a horizontal direction, the guiding hole 18 is communicated with the inner wall of the cylinder 12, a first slider 19 and a second slider 20 are respectively arranged in the left end and the right end of the guiding hole 18, a first inclined plane portion 21 is arranged at the left end of the first slider 19, a second inclined plane portion 22 is arranged at the right end of the second slider 20, the inclined direction of the first inclined plane portion 21 is parallel to the inclined direction of the second inclined plane portion 22, one end of a guiding rod 23 is connected with the upper portion of the right end of the first slider 19, an inclined hole 24 is formed in the cylinder 13 in a penetrating manner, the other end of the guiding rod 23 passes through the inclined hole 24 to be connected with the lower portion of the left end of the second slider 20, the right end of the first slider 19 and the left end of the second slider 20 are both located in the guiding hole 18, and the left end of the first slider 19 extends out of the cylinder 12, the right end of the second slide block 20 extends out of the cylinder 12, and both the first slide block 19 and the second slide block 20 can move left and right along the guide hole 18;
a baffle 25 is arranged on the outer wall of the second sliding block 20, a fourth spring 26 is sleeved on the second sliding block 20, one end of the fourth spring 26 is connected with the baffle 25, and the other end of the fourth spring 26 is connected with the outer wall of the cylinder 12;
the upper end of the bottom plate 8 is provided with a sleeve 27, the cylinder 12 is located in the sleeve 27, the sleeve 27 with be provided with a movable cylinder 28 between the cylinder 12, the outer wall of the movable cylinder 28 with the inner wall of the sleeve 27 contacts, the movable cylinder 28 can move up and down in the sleeve 27, the left and right sides of the inner wall bottom of the movable cylinder 28 are provided with a first pressing block 29 and a second pressing block 30 respectively, the right end of the first pressing block 29 is provided with a third inclined surface part 31, the left end of the second pressing block 30 is provided with a fourth inclined surface part 32, the first inclined surface part 21 is matched with the third inclined surface part 31, the second inclined surface part 22 is matched with the fourth inclined surface part 32, and the upper end of the movable cylinder 28 is separated from the pressing plate 9 by a preset distance.
The working principle and the beneficial technical effects of the technical scheme are as follows: when the pressing plate 9 is subjected to a great impact force, the pressing plate 9 is pressed and moves downwards violently, the second spring 14 and the third spring 15 are in a compressed state, so that the rigidity of the anti-pressing structure is greatly increased, and the anti-pressing structure resists external force, when the impact force is great, the pressing plate 9 moves downwards continuously, the pressing plate 9 presses the moving cylinder 28 downwards, the moving cylinder 28 drives the first pressing block 29 and the second pressing block 30 to move downwards along the sleeve 27, the cylinder 13 moves upwards through the matching of the first inclined plane part 21 and the third inclined plane part 31, the second inclined plane part 22 and the fourth inclined plane part 32, the first sliding block 19, the guide rod 23 and the second sliding block 20 move along the guide hole 18, through the matching of the guide rod 23 and the inclined hole 24 on the cylinder 13, and because the pressing plate 9 and the cylinder 13 approach simultaneously, the second spring 14 and the third spring 15 are in a compressed state, so that the rigidity of the middle part of the pressing plate 9 is increased violently at a nonlinear speed, thereby protecting the fragile part of the middle position, improving the shock resistance of the bat sleeve and playing a role in protecting the table tennis bat.
