Abstract

The invention relates to a table tennis bat handle, which comprises a bat handle body; the racket handle body is provided with wear-resistant grains, the right side of the racket handle body is provided with a connecting rod, the connecting rod is fixed in a bottom sliding groove of a racket plate in a sliding mode and is fastened and fixed through a locking bolt, and a telescopic spring is arranged between the racket handle body and the racket plate; each telescopic spring is parallel to the connecting rod, one end of each telescopic spring is fixedly connected with the racket handle body, and the other end of each telescopic spring is fixedly connected with the bottom of the racket plate. The invention prevents hands from being rubbed, is convenient to replace and has stable structure.

Technical Field
The invention relates to the technical field of table tennis bats, in particular to a table tennis bat handle.
Background
The table tennis bat is one of common sports equipment, the table tennis is also common sports in daily life of people, and the handle of the common table tennis bat is usually made of simple wood.
In life, no matter common individuals play table tennis or athletes train the table tennis skills collectively, the table tennis bat needs to be held by hands for a long time, the loss of the table tennis bat is large, and meanwhile, the habit of users cannot be matched.
Disclosure of Invention
The invention aims to provide a table tennis bat handle, which comprises a bat handle body; the racket handle body is provided with wear-resistant grains, the right side of the racket handle body is provided with a connecting rod, the connecting rod is fixed in a bottom sliding groove of a racket plate in a sliding mode and is fastened and fixed through a locking bolt, and a telescopic spring is arranged between the racket handle body and the racket plate; each telescopic spring is parallel to the connecting rod, one end of each telescopic spring is fixedly connected with the racket handle body, and the other end of each telescopic spring is fixedly connected with the bottom of the racket plate.
Preferably, the number of the locking bolts is more than or equal to 2 and the locking bolts are all fixed in the groove of the racket plate.
Preferably, the racket plate is provided with anti-abrasion pads on both sides.
Preferably, the end part of the racket handle body is provided with a hanging ring.
Compared with the prior art, the invention has the beneficial effects that: can be through dismantling the locking bolt to realize changing the part that needs to be changed, be equipped with the abrasionproof pad, prevent to grind the hand, owing to be equipped with expanding spring, guarantee to stabilize tensile force simultaneously, be equipped with rings, can pull up the couple when finishing, hang the racket.

The racket handle body 1 is provided with wear-resistant grains 2, the right side of the racket handle body is provided with a connecting rod 3, the connecting rod 3 is fixed in a sliding groove at the bottom of a racket plate 4 in a sliding manner and is locked and fixed through a locking bolt 6, and a telescopic spring 5 is arranged between the racket handle body 1 and the racket plate 4; wherein each expansion spring 5 is parallel to the connecting rod 3, one end of each expansion spring is fixedly connected with the racket handle body 1, the other end of each expansion spring is fixedly connected with the bottom of the racket plate 4,
the number of the locking bolts is equal to 2 and the locking bolts are all fixed in the grooves 7 of the racket plate 4; wherein the two sides of the racket plate 4 are provided with anti-wear pads 8.
