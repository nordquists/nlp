Abstract

The invention relates to the technical field of sports equipment, and discloses a table tennis bat with a right-angle handle, which comprises a handle, wherein the lower end of the handle is fixedly connected with a carbon fiber bottom plate, the side wall of the carbon fiber bottom plate is fixedly connected with nitrocellulose plastic, the inner wall of the carbon fiber bottom plate is provided with a counterweight hole, the side wall of the carbon fiber bottom plate is provided with a screw hole, the side wall of the carbon fiber bottom plate is provided with a plate body mechanism, when the table tennis bat is used, a first fixing screw is detached from the screw hole, a counterweight block is inserted into the counterweight hole, after the weight is adjusted to a proper weight, a first protection strip is attached to a plate body, and a first fixing screw is screwed into the; the second fixing rod and the threaded rod are reset, the second fixing screw is detached, the adjusting handle is adjusted to the thickness of the hand, and the second fixing screw is reset, so that the purpose of adjusting the thickness of the handle according to habits of a user is achieved.

Technical Field
The invention relates to the technical field of sports tools, in particular to a table tennis bat with a right-angle handle.
Background
At present, table tennis is deeply popular with the masses in China and becomes one of the most extensive sports, not only because it can exercise body, build body, enrich life, add fun, the characteristics of the circular racket are: the center of gravity of the batting is close to the handle, the resistance arm is short, the starting is rapid, the regulation is flexible, the control is stable, and the square racket has the characteristics that: the center of gravity of a batting is far away from a bat handle, the resistance arm is long, the acceleration is large, the natural batting power is larger, the traditional bat is in a left-right symmetrical and balanced shape no matter in a round shape or a square shape, however, most people who seek the batting characteristics of the special-shaped bat at home and abroad have the characters that some people change the board surface or the board handle of the traditional bat, such as a violin type with changed bat surface shape, and some people are always researching an asymmetrical bat, and a water drop type with changed bat surface and handle angles at foreign countries and a Y-7 type and a 96 type at home are commonly called as a crooked handle; the racket is characterized by also comprising a 'smooth-holding type' mode and the like, wherein the racket face angle and the racket handle shape are completely changed, and if the racket shape is changed without changing the 'holding method', the change mainly changes the 'hitting center' of the racket; the bat handle is changed in shape, the 'holding method' is changed, the use mode of hitting balls can be obviously changed, but the common bat has no functions of adjusting the angle and the weight of the bat, the effect suitable for all people cannot be achieved due to less production, and the outer skin is very easy to damage when the bat is used, so that the loss of the bat is accelerated.
Disclosure of Invention
Technical problem to be solved
Aiming at the defects of the prior art, the invention provides a table tennis bat with a right-angle handle, which solves the problems that the conventional angle and the conventional weight of a user cannot be adjusted and the abrasion in use cannot be reduced in the background technology.
(II) technical scheme
In order to achieve the purposes of adjusting the angle of the grip, the weight of the racket and reducing the abrasion, the invention provides the following technical scheme: a table tennis bat with a right-angle handle comprises a handle, wherein a carbon fiber bottom plate is fixedly connected to the lower end of the handle, nitrocellulose plastic is fixedly connected to the side wall of the carbon fiber bottom plate, a counterweight hole is formed in the inner wall of the carbon fiber bottom plate, a screw hole is formed in the side wall of the carbon fiber bottom plate, and a plate body mechanism is arranged on the side wall of the carbon fiber bottom plate;
the plate body mechanism comprises a balancing weight, a fixing screw, a first protection strip and a second protection strip, the second protection strip is fixedly connected to the carbon fiber bottom plate and the side wall of the nitrocellulose plastic, the first protection strip is rotatably connected to the second protection strip through a bearing, the first fixing screw penetrates through the first protection strip and is in threaded connection with a screw hole, and the upper end of the handle is fixedly connected with a right-angle mechanism;
the right angle mechanism includes fixed plate, dead lever one, bull stick, turns round cap, dead lever two, regulation handle, threaded rod, fixed orifices, fixed screw two, fixed plate fixed connection is in the upper end of handle, the fixed orifices has been seted up to the lateral wall of fixed block, the fixed plate passes through the bearing and is connected with the bull stick rotation, the outer wall fixedly connected with dead lever one of bull stick, the outer wall of dead lever one has cup jointed the regulation handle, dead lever two runs through fixed orifices, fixed block and dead lever one, turn round the lower extreme of cap and the top fixed connection of threaded rod.
Preferably, the number of the carbon fiber bottom plate and the number of the nitrocellulose plastic are both two.
Preferably, the number of the screw holes is two.
Preferably, the number of the weight ports is five.
Preferably, the threaded rod penetrates through the first fixing rod and the fixing block, and the threaded rod and the first fixing rod are in threaded connection with the inner wall of the fixing block.
Preferably, the first fixing rod is fixedly connected with the adjusting handle through a second fixing screw.
Preferably, the number of the fixing holes is three.
Compared with the prior art, the invention provides a table tennis bat with a right-angle handle, which has the following beneficial effects:
(1) when the weight-adjustable plate is used, the first fixing screw is detached from the screw hole, the counterweight block is inserted into the counterweight hole, the proper weight is adjusted, the first protection strip is attached to the plate body, and the first fixing screw is screwed into the screw hole, so that the purpose of adjusting the weight according to the habit of a user is achieved.
(2) According to the invention, the second fixing rod penetrates through the first fixing hole, the first fixing block and the first fixing rod, the threaded rod penetrates through the first fixing rod and the first fixing rod, the threaded rod and the first fixing rod are in threaded connection with the inner wall of the first fixing block, the top end of the threaded rod is fixedly connected with the twisting cap, the threaded rod can be pulled out by twisting the twisting cap, the second fixing rod is pulled out, four different angles can be adjusted due to the four fixing holes formed in the fixing plate, the second fixing rod is inserted into the fixing hole after the second fixing rod is adjusted to the angle of a hand, the threaded rod is inserted into the inner walls of the first fixing rod and the first fixing rod after the second fixing rod penetrates through the fixing plate, the fixing block and the first fixing rod, and the nut is twisted to reversely twist, so that.
(3) According to the invention, the second fixing rod and the threaded rod are reset, the second fixing screw is detached, the adjusting handle is adjusted to the thickness of the hand, and the second fixing screw is reset, so that the purpose of adjusting the thickness of the handle according to the habit of a user is achieved.
