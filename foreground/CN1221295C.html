Abstract

The present invention relates to a table tennis bat which is composed of a hitting plate and a bat handle, wherein the bat handle can be composed of an extension section in a hitting plate shape and a handle sleeve undetachably installed on the extension section. The bat is provided with a center hole along a longitudinal shaft line of the bat according to the present invention, the center hole straightly extends to the front area of a bat handle from the back end of the bat handle, a pipe with smaller diameter is concentrically inserted in the hole to be used as a core and is fixed in the front area of the bat handle, and an annular gap formed between the hole and the pipe is sealed by an elastic sealing ring on the back end of the bat handle. The bat of the present invention can beneficially damp the vibration transferred on a hand during the hitting.
