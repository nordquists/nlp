Abstract

Disclosed is a novel competitive ball game, in particular to a ground ping ball competition court which is composed of a net (1) and a score internal field (2); the length of court is 8 meters, and the width is 3 meters for single game and is 3.4 meters for double game; the height of net equals to 0.75 meter. The competition field of the ground ping game is larger than that of ping pang and smaller than that of tennis. The ground ping ball competition court has the advantages of proper movement range, easy learning, easy exercising, lively force exerting, more rounds, adjustable amount of exercise, less field limits, slower ball speed, more spinning variations, having more skillful requirements and being suitable for men and women, old and young to exercise.
