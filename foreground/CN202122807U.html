Abstract

The utility model discloses a table tennis bat with an improved handle, which comprises a batting plate 1, a bat grab handle 2, a finger hole 3 and a convex block 6, wherein the finger hole 3 is positioned in front of the grab handle 2, the convex block 6 is positioned above the grab handle 2, and the table tennis bat is characterized in that the vertex 9 of the finger hole 3 is positioned on the lower side of the longitudinal center axis 7 of a bat batting region, the vertexes 8 of a groove are positioned in different positions respectively for forming the different bat holding angles, the bat structure is suitable for fast break, combination of fast break and arc ring and arc ring, and the finger hole 3 can enable three fingers to penetrate simultaneously. The structure can not only enable the bat batting region to be closer to the extension line of the forearm when the bat is held so as to improve the force transfer efficiency, but also eliminate the twisting of the joint during the use of the bat; compared with the existing bat, the table tennis bat is more in line with the physiologic structure of a human body, conductive to improving the athletic performance and the pleasure in sports and simple to produce, and can enable the use of various technologies to be more free.
