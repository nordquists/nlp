Abstract

The utility model discloses a table tennis bat with a reinforced finger hole on a ball hitting board. The table tennis bat comprises the ball hitting board 1, a grab handle sheet 2 and the finger hole on the ball hitting board and is characterized in that a reinforcement device 4 for improving the intensity of the ball hitting board is arranged around the finger hole 3. According to the problems of low force and elasticity of the bat with the finger hole, a large amount of longitudinal fibers of a bottom board are cut due to the finger hole, and the intensity of the bottom board is greatly reduced, so that the ball is not hit powerfully, and the performance of materials cannot be exerted; and the reinforcement device 4 directly provides an attachment support point for the cut fibers around the whole finger hole 3, so that the bottom board is not collapsed when the ball is hit, the ball hitting force can be guaranteed, and the performance of the materials can be exerted, the innovation of the ergonomics direction in the field of the table tennis bats can be survived in the market, and the technological advancement and the development and innovation of table tennis sports equipment can be promoted powerfully.
