DE10033236A1 - Method and appliance for delaying degasification involve container with top and bottom parts, valve flow duct as condenser. - Google Patents

Abstract

The method and appliance involve a container (1) with top (3) and bottom (2) parts detachable from each other for inserting and removing a table tennis bat (10) ,and for joining them tightly by exerting excess pressure. A valve (15) is attached to a source of pressurized gas and has a flow duct directly next to it leading to the interior of the container. The duct acts as condenser for extra solvent and contains absorbent material.
