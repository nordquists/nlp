DE10036477A1 - Playing surface for table tennis bat; has sponge underlay glued upper rubber having knobs, with glue only applied at adhesive points between knobs and sponge underlay - Google Patents

Abstract

The playing surface has a sponge underlay (2a) glued to an upper rubber (2b). The glued side of the upper rubber has knobs (4). Adhesive points (3) are formed only between the knobs and the sponge underlay, while the remaining surface of the sponge underlay is free of adhesive. Preferably, the adhesive points are formed with a quick-action adhesive. An Independent claim is included for a method to manufacture a playing surface for a table tennis bat.
