Abstract

The table tennis bat with one or more layers incorporates adhesive means which comprise at least a nonreactive adhesive and/or a reactive adhesive, and serve for joining adjacent layers or adjacent sub-layers within a layer to one another. An Independent claim is also included for a method for producing table tennis bat. It is characterized by the fact that nonreactive and/or reactive adhesives are used for joining layers or sub-layers to one another.
