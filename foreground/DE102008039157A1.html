DE102008039157A1 - Cover for handle of table tennis bat, has layer provided between another layer and handle, where former layer has pores containing gas, and protective layer arranged between layers counteracts leakage of gas from pores of former layer - Google Patents

Abstract

The cover has a layer (2) for directly contacting a table tennis ball, and another layer (6) provided between the former layer and a handle (20) of a table tennis bat, where the latter layer has pores containing gas. A protective layer (10) is arranged between the former layer and the latter layer and counteracts the leakage of the gas from the pores of the latter layer, where the protective layer is a nano-seal and is limited at the latter layer. The former layer is directly connected i.e. pasted, with the protective layer. An independent claim is also included for a method for manufacturing a cover for a handle of a table tennis bat.
