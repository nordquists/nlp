Abstract

The table tennis racket comprises two identical portions, a hollow inside portion which is reinforced with the ribs, and a handle. The slip-resistance striking surface (4) of the racket is made of weather-proof thermoplastic elastomer, and several conical/rounded capped knobs (7) are formed on the slip-resistance striking surface. The base diameter of the knob is greater than 1.5 times the diameter of striking surface, and the length of the knob is half the thickness of the striking surface.
