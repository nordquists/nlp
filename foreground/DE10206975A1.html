Abstract

The standard table tennis bat is assembled of a short straight handle and a main plate of a rounded shape, both parts designed in order to be as lightweight as possible but still vibration resistant. The main area of the new design is made of various layers and incorporates at least one layer provided with a pattern of holes which can either be distributed across the whole surface or arranged in a particular area only. All openings can be of the same size or differ in their dimensions in order to achieve the required effect.
