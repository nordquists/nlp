Abstract

The cuts in the grip can have one of several orientations e.g. one cut at right angles to the grip centreline or in line with this centreline. The grip can also have one or more cuts in its endface away from the blade in which case the cuts lie at right angles to the plane of the blade. The grip further can incorporate at least two cuts in its end in which the cuts are parallel to the centreline of the grip while one of the cuts is parallel with the plane of the blade and the other is at right angles to the plane of the blade.
