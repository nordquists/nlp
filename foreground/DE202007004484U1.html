Abstract

The table (15) has components such as two-piece playing board (1), rollable or stationary underbody (16) and a table tennis ball. The individual components are designed as bright or illuminated active and passive structures. The playing board is made of a transparent or semi-transparent plastic material. The board is illuminated by illuminants such as ultra violet (UV) illuminants (2) and RGB light emitting diode (LED) illuminants (6).
