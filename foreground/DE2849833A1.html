Abstract

the new style table tennis bat (2) is made without any projecting handle and has a striking surface of the same size as that of an ordinary bar or of different size. The shape of the bat may be either symmetrical or asymmetrical and the bat may have built-in openings (1) either for all the fingers together or for each finger separately or a mixture of the two types. The bat may be produced from any material which has the required strength or may be made from a combination of different materials, and it can be covered by any type of covering obtainable through the trade either with the same covering on both sides or with different types of coverings on either side.
