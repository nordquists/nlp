Abstract

In order to provide a table tennis test bat collection having a large number of possible combinations of bat wood/covering, a plurality of bat woods (1) and a plurality of coverings (2) are provided. The coverings (2) are each coated on their underside with a thin adhesive film (3), the material of which is tear-proof and can only be stretched with greater force than the material of the covering (2), and by means of which the covering (2) can be detachably bonded to the various bat woods (1). <IMAGE>
