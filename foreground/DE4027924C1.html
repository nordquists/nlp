Abstract

A table tennis racket has a flat blade (1) essentially made of wood, coated on both sides with a plastic layer and provided with a handle (2). In order to control more precisely the ball and to play more effectively with such a table tennis racket, the outer circumference of the blade (1) has the shape of the section line of an egg that is pointed on one side, bluntly rounded on the opposite side and cut along its longitudinal central plane. The handle is arranged on the blunt rounded side.
