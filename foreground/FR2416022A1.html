Abstract

The handle is for sports racquets or table tennis bats, and is extended by a wider part that fits over the body of the racquet (3). One or both of the edges (1,2) of this wider part can coincide with the edges of the racquet. The handle can have two wider parts, one fitting on each side of the racquet. The edges of the wider part can have the same or different dimensions and one or both edges can have a bevel.
