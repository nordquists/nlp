Abstract

1,127,473. Table tennis &c. bats. CARLTON TYRE SAVING CO. Ltd. 20 April, 1967 [12 May, 1966], No. 21139/66. Heading A6D. A table tennis or padder bat comprises two members to make up the full thickness of the blade, either or both having a surface with recesses 6 and the two members secured together so that at least one cavity is formed within the blade. The bat may be made of plywood or moulded plastics and steel slugs may be placed in the cavities to weight the bat.
