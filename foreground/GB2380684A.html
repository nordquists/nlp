Abstract

A sweat absorbent grip for a table tennis bat comprising a hollow or recess in the handle containing an absorbent sponge pad. The recess is preferably rectangular or oval in shape, and the sponge pad is shaped to fit. The pad may be self adhesive for easy replacement.

The benefit of using "Maxi grip" is that you gain maximum grip without adding to the thickness of the handle.
Using the normal sweat tape that wraps around the handle increases the thickness of the handle and causes the player to change his grip.
"MAXI GRIP" enables the player to maintain an orthodox grip whilst absorbing sweat from the hand, especially important for players with small hands.
