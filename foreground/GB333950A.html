Abstract

333,950. Rimmer, H. A., and Blossom, J. B. May 28, 1929. Battledores and the like; projecting and catching appliances. A game appliance for use in projecting and catching shuttlecocks, tailed-balls, balls or other missiles comprises a bat member a held firmly against the palm of the player's hand by a band or strap b that also serves to carry a cup or other receptacle c. In a modification, the cup is connected to the bat by a stem that passes between the player's fingers and enables the band to be dispensed with.
