592,005. Table tennis bats. WRIGHT, W. Oct. 26, 1944, No. 20862. [Class 132 (ii)] A table tennis bat comprises playing surfaces b b of resilient material, the margins of which are extended and moulded about the edge of the blade to form a protective covering. A single playing surface may be used, the margin being extended about the edge of the blade and moulded to overlap the reverse side, and the moulded portion may be enlarged as at d<1>.
