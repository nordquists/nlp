Abstract

667,020. Table-tennis bats. SMALL, J. Feb. 22, 1950 [Feb. 19, 1949], No. 4576/49. Class 132 (ii). The handle 3 of a table tennis bat comprises a lamina 2 of plywood formed with a hole 4 to take a disc-shaped weight 5 lying in the plane of the blade of the bat and held in position by two wooden parts 6 in order that the point of balance of the bat may be where the handle joins the blade.
