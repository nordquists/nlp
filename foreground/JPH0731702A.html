JPH0731702A - Racket for table tennis - Google Patents

Abstract

PURPOSE:To improve a hitting feel and to make the racket easy to use by forming a main body member of a wooden sheet and a laminate formed by laminating plural sheets of laminated fiber sheets which are union cloth of fully arom. polyester fibers and carbon fibers. CONSTITUTION:The main body member 4 of the racket for table tennis consisting of a ball fitting part and a grip part is made into a laminated structure consisting of a wooden board 7 existing in a central part, the fiber sheets 8 which are the union cloth of the fully arom. polyester fibers and the carbon fibers stuck to both front and rear surfaces of this wooden board 7, and further, wooden sheets 9 stuck to the surfaces thereof to make laminated structure. The laminated structure is constituted by laminating one to plural sheets of the layers of the fiber sheets 8 and the wooden sheets 9. Rubber 6 is stuck at need to the front and rear surfaces of the ball fitting part.
