일반적으로 탁구 라켓은 도 1에서 보는 바와 같이 탁구 라켓의 기본 틀을 구성하는 라켓 나무(2)와 라켓 러버(고무와 스폰지가 결합된 부품을 통칭)(3)로 이루어지고, 상기 라켓 나무(2)와 라켓 러버(3)를 접착하기 위하여 탁구 라켓용 접착제(4)를 사용한다.In general, a table tennis racket is composed of a racket wood (2) and a racket rubber (collectively referred to parts rubber and sponge) (3) constituting the basic frame of the table tennis racket, as shown in Figure 1, the racket wood (2 ) And the racket rubber (3) is used for the table tennis racket adhesive (4).
종래에는 이러한 탁구 라켓과 라켓 러버를 접착시키기 위해서 휘발성 유기 용제가 다량 함유된 용제형 접착제가 사용되었다. 그러나 이러한 용제형 접착제는 벤젠, 톨루엔, 자일렌 등과 같은 독성 유기 용제를 함유하여, 탁구 선수가 경기 전 라켓 보호를 위하여 박리해 놓은 라켓 나무(2)와 탁구 라켓용 러버(3)를 탁구 라켓용 접착제(4)를 사용하여 접착하면, 경기 중 유기 용제가 휘발되면서 탁구 라켓용 러버를 팽윤시켜 러버가 부드러워지게 된다. 이렇게 탁구 라켓용 러버가 부드러워지면 탁구공을 타격할 때 타구의 안정성이 증가하여 컨트롤성이 좋아지고, 타구의 스피드가 증가하는 효과(일명 스피드 글루 효과)를 얻게 된다. Conventionally, a solvent type adhesive containing a large amount of volatile organic solvents has been used to bond such ping pong rackets and racket rubbers. However, these solvent-based adhesives contain toxic organic solvents such as benzene, toluene, xylene, etc., so that the racket wood (2) and the table tennis racket rubber (3) that the table tennis player peeled off for the protection of the racket before the game can be used for the table tennis racket. Bonding using the adhesive 4 causes the rubber for the ping-pong racket to swell while the organic solvent is volatilized during the game, thereby softening the rubber. When the rubber for table tennis rackets becomes soft, the stability of the batting is increased when the table tennis ball is hit, and the control is improved, and the speed of the batting is increased (aka speed glue effect).

The International Table Tennis Association, however, has banned the use of adhesives containing organic solvents that could cause a speed glue effect from September 1, 2008 to prevent athletes from being exposed to toxic organic solvents.

When the content of the monomer having a carboxyl group is less than 0.1 part by weight, the cohesive force is low, and thus the adhesive strength is decreased. When the content of the monomer having the carboxyl group is more than 2.0 part by weight, the viscosity of the monomer is not easily controlled during synthesis and the physical properties of the adhesive change with time.
