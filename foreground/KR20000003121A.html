Abstract

PURPOSE: A ping-pong racket is provided not to show fingers outside when gripping the ping-pong racket. CONSTITUTION: The ping-pong racket comprises:a grip installed on the back face of a racket plate; and about 200 holes of which diameter is around 7mm, installed on the front face of the racket plate.
