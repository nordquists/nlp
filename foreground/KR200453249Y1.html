Description

Device for cutting rubber of table-tennis racket
The present invention relates to a table tennis racket rubber cutting device, and more particularly to a table tennis racket rubber cutting device for cutting the cut portion of the rubber attached to one or both sides of the table tennis racket.
In general, a ping pong racket is composed of a racket main body having a grip and a rubber fixed to a rubber mounting surface that is a hitting surface of the racket main body.
The rubber is composed of a base of a synthetic resin material directly attached to the surface of the racket striking portion, and a rubber member having a plurality of protrusions protrudingly formed on the upper surface of the base and attached to the upper surface of the base.
In addition, the rubber is frequently used as a consumable item for a player who participates in a table tennis game, and is generally fixed to the hitting portion of the racket using a double-sided adhesive film or a solid adhesive.
However, when the rubber is attached to the hitting portion of the racket, a cutout portion protruding out of the hitting portion is generated, and the hitting portion of the racket is curved to generate a cutout portion when attaching a rubber of a square shape.
Therefore, the cut portion of the rubber is cut by hand with a knife or scissors, and the cut surface is not smooth, and often the case of damaging the rubber by mistake frequently during the cutting and the user is often injured.
In particular, when the cut surface of the rubber is cut unevenly, there is a problem that reduces the aesthetics for the user.
Therefore, since rubbers need to be frequently exchanged and used as short-lived consumables, there is an urgent need for a cutting device for quickly, without mistakes and smoothly and neatly cutting the rubber when attaching and cutting the rubber to the racket striking part. .
An object of the present invention for solving the above problems is to provide a table tennis racket rubber cutting device for cutting the cut portion of the rubber attached to one side or both sides of the table tennis racket without mistakes and smoothly and neatly.
