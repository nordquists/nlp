Abstract

PURPOSE: A pen-holder type table tennis racket stably maintaining the gripping state is provided to use a concavo-convex unit on a body or a gripping unit. CONSTITUTION: A pen-holder type table tennis racket includes a body(10), a gripping unit(20), and a concavo-convex unit(30). Rubber(11) attached to the front or both surfaces of the body. The gripping unit is protruded from the body. The concavo-convex unit is installed on the body or the gripping unit for preventing the slipping.
