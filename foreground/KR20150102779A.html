Abstract

The present invention relates to a table tennis racket and, more specifically, to a hand-wearable racket enabling a user to toss a ball, such as table tennis balls for entertainment, to another party while wearing the racket directly on a hand like a glove.

As described above, the racket of the present application is convenient, simple, and can induce an interest even for a beginner, and can contribute to the popularization of table tennis as a sport for everyday life.
