SHAKEHAND TYPE PINGPONG RACKET Improved Shake Hand Type Table Tennis Racket
The present invention relates to a shake hand type table tennis racket, and more particularly to a shake hand type table tennis racket having an improved grip portion.
In general, the shake hand type table tennis racket is composed of a double-sided hitting portion on both sides of which rubber 1 is attached, and a grip portion 2 in which a user can grip the racket, as shown in Fig. 1, 2, the index finger is supported by the tip of the index finger, and the thumb, the finger, and the little finger are grasped by gripping the grip portion 2 to grip the rubber 1 By playing the table tennis ball by selectively using the attached double-headed hitting parts to the side of the opponent side by playing the table tennis ball, the batting part capable of hitting the table tennis ball is formed on both sides so that the backhand or forehand attack, It can easily proceed.
However, in the case of the conventional registered utility model No. 428238, the problem of the conventional shake hand type racket described above, that is, the phenomenon that the grip of the user who grips the grip portion is almost entirely brought into close contact with the grip portion, And when the attack is switched from the backhand to the forehand due to the slippage, there is a problem in that the wrist in the turning of the racket is shifted to the wrist or the problem in which the table tennis ball is not hit correctly at the hit point It is a fact that I can not. Accordingly, it is an object of the present invention to solve at least the above-mentioned problems, and it is an object of the present invention to provide a grip portion in which two or more front or side axes of a grip portion are provided, thereby forming a void space between a hand and a grip portion, The present invention provides a shake hand type table tennis racket.
According to an aspect of the present invention, there is provided a shake hand type table tennis racket comprising: a racket body; Two hit portions on both sides of the racket body with rubber attached thereto; And a grip portion extending to a lower side of the racquet body, wherein at least two front axes or side axes of the grip portion are present.
The apparatus may further include a water support portion formed at an end of the grip portion to support a user's hand when gripping the grip portion.
The grip portion may further include a projection formed on an outer surface of the grip portion so that a user's finger is held while being gripped by the grip portion.
In order to prevent the racket from shaking when the grip portion is gripped, the lower end of the hit portion may be in close contact with the grip between the thumb and the index finger, thereby preventing the racket from swinging.
According to the present invention, since there are two or more front axes or side axes of the grip portion, an empty space is formed between the palm and the grip due to the two side axes of the grip portion when the grip portion is gripped by the hand, The sweat generated in the adjacent palm can be easily evaporated due to the wind which flows through the formed space and the slip is minimized.
In addition, since the index finger is closely attached to the rear portion of the racket body and the grip portion of the shake hand type racket is gripped with the thumb, the stop, the finger, and the little finger separately, Thereby preventing the hands from being disengaged from the grip portion, thereby improving the aggressiveness due to the hit of the table tennis ball.
Further, since protrusions are formed on the outer surface of the grip portion for gripping the racquet body to support the fingers, both of the both surfaces are used. Thus, the frictional action of the protrusions facilitates switching between the backhand and the forehand So that even beginners can easily enjoy the table tennis game using the double-sided method of the shake hand type racket, and the aggressiveness due to the hit of the table tennis ball is improved by reducing the shaking.
In addition, since the lower end portion of the hit portion is in close contact with the grip between the thumb and the index finger to prevent the racket from shaking, the anti-shake portion is formed at the close contact portion of the lower end portion of the hit portion, Beginners as well as skilled people can easily switch to a straight line and can play a more stable and stable batting.

As shown in the drawing, a shake hand type table tennis racket having an improved grip portion according to the present invention has a conventional racket body 10, two pivot portions 11 And a grip portion 13 extending to the lower side of the racquet body 10 and wherein at least two of the side axes a of the grip portions 13 or the front axes c of the grip portions 13 are present .
The presence of two or more side axes (a) of the grip portion (13) means that the side axes (a) of the grip portions are bent at least twice.
The presence of two or more front axes (c) of the grip portion (13) means that the front axis (c) of the grip portion is bent more than twice.
In the shake hand type table tennis racket according to the present invention, the space portion 80 is formed in the grip portion bent by the presence of two or more side axes a or front axes c of the grip portion 13 The grip portion and the palm of the hand are minimized by minimizing the heat generated when the palm is in close contact due to the space portion 80 formed in the bent grip portion where the palm does not come in contact with the hand when gripping the grip portion by hand, Minimize it to enable accurate and strong batting.
The side shaft a of the grip portion 13 is bent twice or more on the side shaft b of the tip portion 11 so that the flow of air through the space portion 80 formed in the bent grip portion becomes smooth, The sweat of the sweat is rapidly evaporated, so that the slip phenomenon of the grip is minimized.
Likewise, the front axis c of the grip portion 13 is bent twice or more on the front axis d of the tip portion 11, so that the flow of air through the space portion 80 formed in the bent grip portion And the discharged sweat is rapidly evaporated, so that the slip phenomenon of the grip is minimized.
Meanwhile, the shake hand type table tennis racket having the improved grip portion according to the present invention includes a water support portion 20 formed at an end of the grip portion 13 to support a user's hand at the time of gripping the grip portion 13 . That is to say, the grip portion 13 is provided so as to firmly support the user's hand at the time of gripping the grip portion 13, that is, the portion of the user's finger that reaches the wrist from the tip of the little finger to prevent the hand from being separated from the grip portion 13 And a water support portion 20 at an end thereof.
Further, the shake hand type table tennis racket having an improved grip portion according to the present invention may further include a protruding portion 30 formed on the outer surface of the grip portion 13 so as to support a user's finger when gripping the grip portion 13 . That is, the protruding portion 30 is formed on the outer surface of the grip portion 13 to prevent the user's finger and the grip portion 13 from slipping.
In the shake hand type table tennis racket according to the present invention, the lower end of the hit portion 11 is brought into close contact with the grip between the thumb and the index finger to prevent the racket from shaking, The anti-shake portion (70) is further formed in the close contact portion of the lower end portion, thereby preventing the racket from being shaken more firmly and having a comfortable feeling of the hand racket and the hand.
