Racket {RACKET}
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION 1. Field of the Invention The present invention relates to a racket, and more particularly to a racket capable of hitting a ball or a shuttlecock such as a table tennis ball, a badminton ball, a tennis ball,
For example, in the case of a table tennis racket used for table tennis, the racket has a narrow swing width due to the characteristics of a narrow table tennis die, so that the resistance of the air is not greatly affected, and the length of the grip is also short In badminton racquets used in badminton sports, a plurality of rows are woven in a lattice form to minimize the resistance of the air, and the length of the grip portion is also long, so that the badminton ball at a distance can be hit without difficulty .
Meanwhile, the conventional racquet has a lot of mobility, so it can be advantageous to hold a strong grip and hit the ball. When the swing speed is increased, there is no way to prevent or minimize the racket from getting out of the hand.
In addition, slippage or the like occurs due to sweat generated during exercise, and the racket may feel uncomfortable due to anxiety that the racket is going to exit the hand during a fast swing.
On the other hand, people whose hands are injured or uncomfortable can not grip racquet properly, which is a great inconvenience to enjoy the exercise.
Accordingly, it is an object of the present invention to provide a racket capable of fast and strong swing while grasping the racket with less force and preventing the racket from escaping.
In order to achieve the above object, the racquet according to the present invention is characterized in that the racket includes at least one grip portion and at least one strip, and the racket includes a body and a grip extending from the body .
In addition, the strip may include a strip fixing unit for wrapping a hand and fixing it to a hand.
The strip may also include a racquet securing portion to prevent the racquet from exiting the strip.
According to the present invention, the strip is connected to the grip portion of the racket, and the strip connected to the hand is fixed to the hand, so that the racket can be easily grasped and the racket is prevented from slipping out of the hand. .
Also, people with impaired or inability to move their fingers, or people whose fingers are damaged or shortened, so that they can not grip the grip at all. I can not get out of it, so I can exercise comfortably and strongly with my racket at will.
In addition, since the racket is not pulled out by gripping and racing the racket with the strip, it is possible to make an advantageous method of gripping the new racket by each user, thereby making it possible to make a more fatal attack to the opponent.
