Abstract

The present invention relates to an elliptical frame, a string provided at a predetermined distance from the inner circumferential surface of the frame, a net in which the string is crossed, a bag extending from one side of the frame, and a grip extending along the length of the bag, And a weight attached to the other end frame head portion of the frame. In the swinging type power swing racket of the present invention, when the racket meets the ball while rotating around the elbow and the wrist, the end of the racket frame is faster than the center of the frame. In other words, since the relative speed varies depending on the position where the frame meets the ball, a weight is inserted into the head portion that gives the maximum speed in the racket by centrifugal force, thereby maximizing the swing power. Also, since only the frame head of the racket is heavy, the overall weight is relatively light, so it can be used for beginners and for ladies.

BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION 1. Field of the Invention [0001] The present invention relates to an attack type power racket for providing an attack type racket having a center of gravity in a frame head portion, and more particularly, to an attack type power swing racket for maximizing centrifugal force when a ball is inserted into a frame head portion.
Many people use tennis, baseball, golf, and other spats using racquets. While enhancing the design of the racquet, it enhances the function and reduces the weight, while using materials that enhance the rigidity and toughness of the durability, or the vibration of each racket is minimized and the ball is easily controlled. .
Generally, a racket includes a head, a shaft, a neck for connecting the head and the shaft, and a grip for wrapping the cloth or leather so that the racket held by the hand does not slip, (Net), which is a part where the ball is impacted, and a head frame, which has a hollow frame structure and strings are struck.
The swing speed of this racket comes from the snap of the wrist which instantly controls the racket, and the racket consisting of a soft, thin shaft and slim head frame is generally used Is preferred. However, while the slender flexible shaft and head frame racket have the advantages of good elasticity, quick operation and control of subtle racket movement, the shock absorbing ability of the racket is weakened, and when the shuttlecock impact, There is a disadvantage that the racket can rotate in the direction of this impact. Therefore, in the case of a slim racket, there is a problem that when the shuttlecock is attempted to be hit by a quick swing such as a smash or a drive, the stability of the shuttle is deteriorated and the accurate control becomes rather difficult or power is not turned on.
In addition, it is possible to increase the power when using a racket made of a head heavy type or a rigid shaft, and to improve the stability at the time of impact of a shuttlecock. However, in order to cope with a rapid airflow, The instability due to the impact of the racket or the impact on the racket is a common problem that must be solved likewise.
A tennis racket and a racket having a balance and center-of-gravity adjusting function are disclosed in Korean Patent Laid-Open Publication No. 1999-007508 (published on February 25, 1999), and Korean Patent Laid-Open Publication No. 2011-0121298 It has been proposed.
The object of the present invention is to provide a racket suitable for play in which aggressiveness is important. More specifically, a metal material having a specific gravity higher than that of the main material of the racket is inserted into the head of the racket, To an outermost power swing racket that increases the instantaneous speed and power of the swing.
In order to achieve the above object, an attack type power swing racket according to the present invention includes an elliptical frame, a string provided at a predetermined distance from the inner circumference of the frame, a net crossing the string, And a weight which is inserted into the other end frame head portion of the frame, including a grip extending along the length direction of the bag portion.
In addition, the weight is fixedly inserted into the molding frame and molded like the frame.
Further, the frame main material is graphite.
Further, the weight is a material having a larger specific gravity than the graphite.
Further, the weighing device is characterized by being a large metal thin plate having a specific gravity larger than that of graphite as a main material of the frame.
In the present invention, the end of the racket frame is faster than the center of the frame when the racket meets the ball while rotating around the elbow and wrist. In other words, since the relative speed varies depending on the position where the frame meets the ball, a weight is inserted into the head portion that gives the maximum speed in the racket by centrifugal force, thereby maximizing the swing power.
Also, since only the frame head of the racket is heavy, the overall weight is relatively light, so it can be used for beginners and ladies.
