KR20180038713A - The assistant for shakehand type pingpong racket - Google Patents

Abstract

The present invention relates to an assisting tool (100) which is used for a shake-hand type table tennis racket having a racket body (10) and a grip part (13) extending from the racket body (10), and characterized by comprising a hand-blade support part (20) formed on the grip part to support a hand-blade of a user while holding the grip part. The assisting tool of the present invention supports the hand-blade of a user to prevent the hand from being separated from the grip part.
