Abstract

The present invention relates to an assistive device (100) used in hand shaking-type ping-pong paddles, including a racket body (10) and a grip part (20) elongated from the racket body (10). The assistive device (100) also includes an extension part having a specific thickness and area to support a hand by preventing shaking through reduction of intervals between the hand and the racket body (10) when gripping a grip part (13), by being coupled to a hand attachment part (110) to which the hand of the racket body (10) is attached.
