Abstract

The present invention relates to an assistant tool (100) used for table tennis rackets, having a racket body (10) and a grip part (20) extending from the racket body (10). To this end, the assistant tool (100) includes an anti-slip part (120) having a groove for minimizing slippage of a hand and a palm due to contact with the grip part (20) when a user grips the grip part (20).
