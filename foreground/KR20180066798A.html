KR20180066798A - Ball storage on-board table tennis racket - Google Patents

Abstract

The present invention relates to a handle-loaded table tennis racket containing: a body; a handle part installed to be connected with one side of the body; and a cylinder having the inner wall of the handle part made of a silicone material; and a lid. The table tennis racket according to the present invention has an effect of allowing the elderly enjoying table tennis not to have to bend backs, so that the elderly can enjoy sports more healthily.
