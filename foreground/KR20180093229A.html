KR20180093229A - Integral a ping-pong paddle - Google Patents

Abstract

The present invention relates to an integrated table tennis paddle. An objective of the present invention is to easily protect the player′s wrist by a wrist protector even when excessively using the wrist to resolve a conventional problem where advanced techniques including various basic movements are difficult to perform without a series of lesson processes for a beginner to play table tennis. According to the present invention, the integrated table tennis paddle without a handle comprises: an integrated paddle body whose interior consists of a glove liner of a microfiber material capable of smoothly enclosing fingers and whose exterior consists of wood and a rubber pad to perform functions of table tennis to freely bend a wrist in horizontal and vertical directions to easily perform forehand and backhand strokes without interference of fingers with a paddle; and a wrist protector formed in a wristband shape to smoothly enclose a wrist for wrist protection to protect the wrist since wrist injuries by the weight of a table tennis paddle may occur during a motion of freely moving the integrated paddle. Therefore, the present invention easily protects a wrist by a wrist protector even when excessively using the wrist to resolve a conventional problem where advanced techniques including various basic movements are difficult to perform without a series of lesson processes for a beginner to play table tennis.
