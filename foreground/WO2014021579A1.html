WO2014021579A1 - Table tennis racket protection cap and tablet tennis racket having same attached thereto - Google Patents

Abstract

An object of the present invention is to prevent an edge of a table tennis racket that includes rubber from being damaged by fixing a tablet tennis racket protection cap, which is formed of an elastic material such as rubber, silicone, or synthetic resin, to the edge of the table tennis racket.

Generally, a ping pong racket comprises a racket wooden board, a sponge, a handle and a rubber. Racket wooden boards are usually made of wood and are mostly inelastic. The rubber is typically fixed to the racket wooden board with an adhesive or with the sponge inserted in the racket wooden board. The rubber attached with the adhesive is separated from the racket wooden board from the edge of the table tennis racket due to the table tennis ball or external impact.
일반적으로 러버는 크게 평면러버, 돌출러버, 및 안티스핀러버 세가지로 나눌 수 있는데, 특히 평면러버와 안티스핀러버는 스폰지 위에 돌기가 형성된 러버가 부착되어서 스폰지와 러버 사이에 빈공간이 형성되고, 이 빈공간이 탁구라켓의 가장자리로 노출된 경우에는 러버는 더욱 쉽게 스폰지로부터 분리될 수 있다.In general, rubber can be classified into three types: flat rubber, protruding rubber, and anti-spin rubber. Especially, flat rubber and anti-spin rubber are attached with rubber with protrusions formed on the sponge. If the empty space is exposed to the edge of the table tennis racket, the rubber can be more easily separated from the sponge.
